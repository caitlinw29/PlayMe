Instructions

* Click anywhere to exit instructions *


Your goal is to collect all 52 cards in the deck!

- Both players start with 26 cards.

- Hit the flip cards button when you are ready to see your cards for the round!

- Whoever has the higher card keeps both cards for the round!

- If you have the same card, it's WAR! Each player will place two more cards down. Then they will flip the second card. Whoever has the higher card will keep ALL of the drawn cards!

- Game ends when one player has all cards!

Have fun! Thanks for playing ♥
